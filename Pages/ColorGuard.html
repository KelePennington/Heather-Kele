  • Color guard can be found in most American colleges, universities, high schools, middle schools, and independent drum corps. Color guard uses various equipment, such as flags, rifles, and sabres, along with dance, to express dynamic passages in the music accompanying the marching band show or winterguard show.

  • The purpose of the color guard is to interpret the music that the marching band or drum corps is playing.

  • Color Guard members spin a 5 to 6 inch metal poles. You get different sizes based on how tall you are. On these metal flags poles are are silks. Silks are what we call the flag fabric that is on the metal pole.

  • Some Color Guards use rifles. Color Gaurd rifles are made out of thick wood.There are many different types and sizes of rifles. They also come in many different colors. To spin with rifles take a lot of hard work and training

  • A lot of Color Guards use sabres.Color Guard Sabres are swords with the ends dulled and are safe for them to use
